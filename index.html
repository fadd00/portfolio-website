ABOUT ME

Hi! My name is Agus Setiawan, but most people call me Ares. I’m a 20-year-old Communication Science student at Sultan Ageng Tirtayasa University, class of 2023, and I’m deeply passionate about photography.
My journey with the camera began back in junior high school. Since then, I’ve explored various photography genres—street, landscape, portrait, and more. Each style has taught me something different, but what fascinates me the most is how every image can hold a feeling, not just a moment. I believe that a good photo isn’t just about what’s visible, but about what’s felt. That’s why I live by this philosophy:
“What’s captured is not just a moment, but a feeling.” I’ve participated in several photography exhibitions, including the Harbufkes x Persona TVP event, and these experiences have only fueled my ambition to grow as a professional photographer. I aim to work with many clients and keep creating visuals that connect with people on a deeper emotional level.
